Serenade puts on popup events showcasing personal and experimental interactive works and videogames.

We've teamed up with our friends Sabby and isyourguy, creator of Inkrunner, to host an in-person venue for Ink Jam 2024.

Ink Jam is a game jam for making games using the ink narrative scripting language created by inkle and used in their games including 80 Days, Heaven's Vault and A Highland Song.

Ink has been used in local games including Ghost Pattern's Wayward Strand and Cecile Richard's Gutless and international games like Sable and Goodbye Volcano High.

Join us at Sabby and make a game using ink.

If you've never used ink before alongside the jam we are running an optional workshop to introduce attendees to ink. If you already know ink you can jump right into working on your game but for those new to ink the workshop will help you get started.

We’ve been inspired by other exhibitions, collectives and venues around Australia and internationally which highlight games that are deeply personal to their creators, games made without commercial intent and games engaging with topics rarely explored within the wider medium. We wanted to bring some of this energy to Sydney. So much more is possible in games and we don't often get to see it here. We want to contribute to changing that.
