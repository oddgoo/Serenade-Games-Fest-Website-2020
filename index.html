Serenade puts on popup events showcasing personal and experimental interactive works and videogames.

Our friends at Static Vision are bringing their Dreamscapes Film Festival to Melbourne's Lido Cinemas. (675 Glenferrie Road Hawthorn).

Serenade have partnered with a collective we look up to and draw inspiration from, Hovergarden, for the Dreamscapes Melbourne Games Space. In the Dreamscapes Games Space you will be able to play a selection of games picked by Serenade & Hovergarden in response to the films in the Dreamscapes lineup.

The new date for the festival is dependent on the end of the current Melbourne lockdown. Please subscribe to Static Vision's newsletter and follow Static Vision's social media accounts (Twitter, Instagram & Facebook) for updates.
