Serenade puts on popup events showcasing personal and experimental interactive works and videogames.

We've teamed up with our friends Pixel Pasture to host a party to celebrate the games created during Game-A-Week Sydney 2024.

Game-A-Week Sydney was an intensive program where participants prototyped one game a week over the course of five weeks in response to a set of prompts designed to encourage creativity and experimentation.
